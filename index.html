Created a community

In the spring of 2011 me & Daniel Knell had the idea of creating a new new tech event. The goals where simple; Offer something for the best and brightest web developers around, a space to share ideas & stimulate them. Two years later London JS has had 42 speakers from all over the world coming to share they knowladge with out community. Somer personal highlights for me have been Paul King, Irene Ros, Addy Osmani, Syd Lawrence, Mark Wubben, Jake Archibald & Andrew Betts. I've watch had the pleasure of watching some of our new faces develop into regulers of the confernce curcit. Seen our attendees get inspired to make some wonderful things.
